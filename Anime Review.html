JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is one of my favourite animes because of all the amazing fight scenes, great story telling and unique art style. JoJo's has a 9.9/10 rating from my own experience, with part 4 being my favourite animated part so far. JoJo's Bizarre Adventure has 5 parts animated so far and part 6 is expected to be announced on 4/4/2021 via the Joestar's Inherited Soul Event. Details on this can be found on the official JoJo website here. This page is in japanese so if you do visit it make sure you have a browser translating plugin installed (if you are using google chrome then it has a default google translate plugin installed). The official announcemment video is on the left. Watch JoJo's Bizarre Adventure on Crunchyroll.
SPOILERS AHEAD!

Part 1: Phantom Blood

Set in the 1880's Phantom Blood is the first part in the series and starts off with George Joestar I, his wife and baby in a carriage crash. A man named Dario Brando planned to steal the valuables from this crash, however just as he did George I mistook the man for someone trying to save him and before passing out asked his name and told him that the Joestars were forever indebted to him.
12 years later a bed-ridden Dario tell his son Dio Brando to go to the Joestar mansion and take the world for everything it has, he had already contacted George Joestar and it was agreeed that Dio would be adopted by him. Shortly after Dio arrives him and Georges son Jonathan Joestar have a fight after multiple fall outs, during this fight some of Dio's blood is splattered onto a strange stone mask and activates it, causing it to fall off the wall it was placed. What Bizarre events will this lead too...

Part 2: Battle Tendency

49 years after the events in Phantom Blood, Jonathan's grandson Joseph Joestar is tasked with defeating the Pillar Men with the assistance of his Hamon instructor Lisa Lisa and William A. Zepelli's grandson. The pillar men are over 3,000 years old and are the creators of the stone masks! Their main goal is to acquire the red stone of Aja, currently in Lisa Lisa's possesion. How will the adventure turn out...

Part 3: Stardust Crusaders

100 years have passed since Jonathan Joestar has died, and now the story is passed to Joseph's grandson Jotaro Kujo. Joseph flies to Japan to see his daughter and her son but is met by a surprise when he arrives. Jotaro is in jail and refuses to leave because he is haunted by an 'evil spirit', Joseph however knows what this 'evil spirit' is and explains a mysterious power a select few have called stands. They are the manifestation of your sould and only those with a strong resolve can withstand their brutal power, whether this powe is used for good or evil is down to the user. Soon after Holly (Jotaro's mum and Joseph's daughter) also gains a stand, however is too weak to handle it so she falls ill. To save her from her death the two must team up with other stand users and defeat the man that haunts their entire bloodline... DIO! Where will this journey lead them...

Part 4: Diamond is Unbreakable

9 years after DIO had been defeated, the adventure is contuined in the town of Morioh. Jotaro Kujo has travelled to Morioh too find the result of Joseph Joestar's affair with Tomoko Higashikata, Josuke Higashikata! Shortly after Jotaro had found him, Josuke's grandfather was murdered by an enemy stand user. Determined to find answers they found the secret of a mysterious arrow that can give people stand abilities. After they had found the person responsible for making new stand users, another threat surfaced in the form of a serial killer that has been living in Morioh for over 15 years! When will his justice come...

Part 5: Golden Wind

After 2 years, Koichi Hirose travels to Italy with Jotaro Kujo and are met with a stand user called Giorno Giovanna, the son of DIO but concieved using Jonathan's body. What are his intentions? Is he evil like his father before him? These are the questions Jotaro wanted to answer, however Giorno was none of these things. Giorno's Dream was pure and simple in thought, he did not want the mafia to sell drugs to children, so he set off on his goal to become a real Gang-Star and take over Passione (the mafia). Why is it so hard to find the boss...

Sword Art Online

Sword Art Online was one of the first animes that I watched and will forever have a specila place in my heart. Despite many anime fans disliking this show, I personally loved it and gave it a 10/10 for a number of reasons, especially because I loved the storyline and how it played out. However the second part of season 2 wasn't as good as the others in my opinion, this is just because I didn't really enjoy the story for that part but that is just personal opinion. So far Sword Art Online has 3 animated seasons, with a film coming out later this year (2021) about Asuna's experience in Aincrad (first season). The announcemment trailer for this is on the left. Watch Sword Art Online on Crunchyroll. SPOILERS AHEAD!

Season 1: Aincrad and Alfheim Online

On November 6 2022 10,000 players were trapped in a Virtual Reality Massively Multiplayer Online (VRMMO) game with no exit option, their only way out: clear all 100 floors on the floating castle. The missing exit button wasnt the only thing keeping them trapped, should the Nerve Gear be removed outside the VR world, then the player will die in real life. If a player dies in-game then they also die in real life. Beta tester 'Kirito' is amongst the first people to clear a floor, and it was at this time he met 'Asuna' and these two would eventually go on to kill the Game Master (GM) and free all surviving players. After escaping Sword Art Online, Kirito sets out to find Asuna in the real world but is instead finds out she has been trapped in yet another game. To free Asuna Kirito wears the nerve gear once more and with the help of his sister 'Leafa' finds and sets free his wife.


Season 2: Gun Gale Online and Alfheim Online

Kirito's help is needed by a government funded organisation when a veteran Sword Art Online player appears in Gun Gale Online and claims that they can kill someone in the real world through the game. Trying to set up his character Kirito is helped by a sniper-specialist called 'Sinon' who helps him track down the killer. When they eventually find this killer it is revealed that it is one of Sinon's friends from school and another accomplice. The second part of this season focuses on Asuna helping a team of patients not likely to survive their treatment in their dreams to be the first to complete the new floor that is being released in Alfheim Online. There is also a smaller part inbetween where Kirito, Asuna and Klein's group travels underneath the world tree to restore the land to its former self.

Season 3: Alicization

Kirito has been taking part in the before mentioned gorvenment funded team and when he has been attacked he is put into the virtual word called 'The Underworld' where he meets Eugeo and learns the laws of the world. The two then set of to defeat 'Administraor' to free Eugeo's childhood friend ALICE from her powers. Although successful Eugeo is killed and Kirito's soul had been damaged, causing him to be paralysed. After this they realise they have created true artificial inteligence through the virtual world, they succeed in doing this and when the United States government find this out, they hire a team in secret to go and retrieve ALICE. The team however are not successful even after trying to get her from inside The Underworld as Asuna and the others come to help The Underwold's people from being destroyed by the dark forces, led by 'Emporer Vector' or Gabriel Miller in real life.

Citrus

Citrus is a romance anime between two step sisters and is not liked by many anime fans for a number of reasons, the main being the theme of sexual assualt in each episode although it never goes to far. I personally really enjoyed this anime and would reccomend watching it if you are interested but if things easily trigger/offend you then perhaps look at the trigger warnings somewhere else as there are quite a few. It starts off with Yuzu's mum moving in with her boyfriend and this mean Yuzu has to move schools, however the chosen one is an all-girls school and immediately upon entering she is warned by the student council president Mei that she is not properly equiped to enter the school. To her surprise it is later revealed that this student is her new step sister and however wrong she thinks it is, ends up falling for her. Overall this anime got a 10/10 because I enjoyed the story and animation. The trailer for this is on the left, but no season 2 has been confirmed yet. Watch Citrus on Crunchyroll

Season 1

New student Yuzu has moved to an all girls school where she has developed what she thinks is love for the student council president who is also her new step sister! Yuzu had tried to forget and accept that it was wrong to love your sister but when Mei kissed her she went back into a state of confusion. Many people do not want the two to get together throughout this season but in the end they do and they are both happy.
